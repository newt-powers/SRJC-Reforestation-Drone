Here's the problem

Wildfires are destroying forests around the world at alarming rates. Not only are these intensified burn events negatively impacting ecosystems but also destroying family’s homes and livelihoods in a flash.

How did this get so bad?

Two things: poor forestry management and climate change. Decades ago, the forestry services focused on putting out every single spot fire leading to an overgrowth now with more fuel for wildfires. Climate change has made certain areas hotter and drier: more wildfire-prone.

So what’s the fix here?

Planting forests that are more resilient to wildfires. This means having less monospecies forests and more biodiversity of plant life to establish stronger roots and reduce total burning of trees. Furthermore, we need to focus no replanting trees in already decimated ecosystems to prevent climate change from impacting further.

But there’s an issue.

Forests are extremely vast. 1000’s of acres of land cannot be reseeded very quickly. So how can we accelerate growing more resilient forests?

Enter our reforestation drone.

This drone can distribute seeds multiple factors faster and with a much wider range than an average group of people can with multiple plant species at semi-regular intervals to promote greater forest growth.

That sounds great, but how will we do that?

We plan on partnering with local and state governments, private landowners, forestry services, and many more to make the reforestation a reality.
